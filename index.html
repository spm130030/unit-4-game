Crystal Collecter

You'll be given a random number at the beginning of the game

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific value to your total score.

You'll win the game by matching your total score to random number, you lose if the total score exceeds the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it.

Each time the game begins, the game will change the values of each crystal.
